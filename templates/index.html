Welcome to the fascinating world of arithmetic

This game aims to mimic the behavior of an abacus (without the beads that is)

The rules are pretty simple:

  • You will be given a series of numeric intervals
  • For each interval you have to type in how many numbers are in that interval

The intervals are of many different types but that's a whole nother story
In this version of the game you will be using the following types of intervals:

  • the open interval: For example, (0,9) has 8 numbers
  • the closed interval: For example, [0,9] has 10 numbers
  • the half-open interval: For example (0,9] has 9 numbers
